Retirement Savings Tips

Start saving early to benefit from compounding interest.

Contribute enough to your employer-sponsored retirement plan to get the full employer match.

Automate your savings by setting up automatic contributions from your paycheck.

Consider increasing your retirement savings rate whenever you receive a pay raise.

Regularly review and adjust your retirement plan as needed.
