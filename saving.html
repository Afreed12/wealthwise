Saving Tips

- Set up automatic transfers to your savings account each payday.

- Create a budget and prioritize saving goals.

- Take advantage of employer-sponsored retirement plans and matching contributions.

- Use apps or tools to track your spending and identify areas to save.

- Consider saving windfalls, such as tax refunds or bonuses, instead of spending them.
